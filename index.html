Location

The house is located in White City Township very close to the intersection of 10600 South and 1300 East (That's 106 blocks south of the Temple in downtown Salt Lake and 13 blocks east). It has a Sandy, Utah mailing address. The rear of the property butts up against Dimple Dell Park. The exact street address will be provided if/when appropriate. Convenience

The house is very conveniently located close to 9 major ski areas, Salt Lake City and the airport, main access roads, Dimple Dell Regional Park, Ski Bus Park & Ride lots, and a wide variety of amenities.

For example, the house is approximately a...

  • 1 minute walk out the backyard to Dimple Dell Regional Park for hiking, jogging and horseback riding.
  • 5 minute walk to a Beans & Brews Coffee House, Chevron gas station, McDonald's, Pizza Hut, bike shop and banking options.
  • 5 minute drive (12 minute walk thru Dimple Dell Park) to a Smith's grocery store, Subway, a great Indian restaurant and 24-hour gym.
  • 8 minute drive to Alta View Hospital and walk-in clinic.
  • 8 minute drive to I-15 for direct access to Salt Lake City (20 minute drive), the airport (25 minute drive) or points south.
  • 10 minute drive to Rio Tinto Stadium for Real Salt Lake professional soccer.
  • 10 minute drive to a Lowes/Walmart complex.
  • 10 to 20 minute drive to almost any restaurant or shopping you can imagine.
  • 27 minute drive on major roadways to Snowbird ski areas lower parking lot. 35 minutes to Alta, just a little further up Little Cottonwood Canyon.
  • 35 minute drive on major roadways to Solitude ski area and 40 minutes to Brighton, further up Big Cottonwood Canyon.
  • 45-50 minute drive to Park City, the Canyons and Deer Valley.
  • 50 to 75 minute drive to Snowbasin and Powder Mountain.
  • 4 hour drive south to Zion National Park.

Layout

The open floorplan style house is approximately 600 sq. ft. It is located at the rear of the property, behind the main house on the circle, and includes a queen size bed, fold out futon and loft area to sleep a maximum of 3 adults and 2 kids.

There is a living room area with a wood burning stove and wall mounted big-screen TV, a dining room table that seats 5, full kitchen with island seating for 3, a full bath with a tiled shower and a small mud/utility room with a stacked washer and dryer.
